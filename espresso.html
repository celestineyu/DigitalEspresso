Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage that originated in Italy. It's made by forcing hot water through finely ground coffee beans at high pressure, resulting in a strong, flavorful shot that's often served in a small cup. Espresso is the foundation for many popular coffee drinks, including lattes, cappuccinos, and Americanos.
