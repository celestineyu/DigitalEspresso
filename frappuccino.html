Frappuccino

Frappuccinos are a popular blended coffee drink that were popularized by the Starbucks coffee chain. They're made by blending a combination of coffee, milk, ice, and flavored syrups, resulting in a creamy, slushy beverage that's perfect for warm weather or as a sweet treat. Frappuccinos come in a wide range of flavors, including mocha, caramel, and vanilla, and can be topped with whipped cream and drizzles of syrup for added indulgence.
