Americano

An Americano is a popular espresso-based drink that originated in Italy. It's made by adding hot water to a shot or two of espresso, resulting in a drink that's similar in strength to drip coffee but with a distinctively different flavor profile. The water helps to dilute the espresso's intense flavor, resulting in a smoother, less bitter taste. Americanos can be enjoyed black or with milk and sugar added to taste. They are a classic choice for coffee lovers who want a simple, no-frills drink that still packs a punch.
