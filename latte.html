Latte

A latte is a popular coffee drink made by combining a shot of espresso with steamed milk and a small amount of foam. The drink is typically served in a large mug and can be customized with a variety of flavorings, such as vanilla or caramel. Lattes are known for their smooth, creamy texture and subtle, nuanced flavors, making them a popular choice for coffee drinkers who enjoy a more mild and indulgent beverage.
